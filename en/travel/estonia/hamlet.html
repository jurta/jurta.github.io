Türisalu cliff is a spectacular place located 20 km (12 mi) from Tallinn, Estonia. The cliff has a height of up to 30 meters (100 ft). It is often named "Hamlet" because a 1964 film adaptation of Shakespeare's play "Hamlet" was filmed here.

This cliff is a popular place for suicides where people ask themselves a question To be or not to be, then decide not to be and jump off the cliff.
